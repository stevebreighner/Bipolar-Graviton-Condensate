How to read the sketch: the warm marker and cool marker are the opposite bipolar faces. When they face each other at moderate distance, the pair attracts. When the translucent shells crowd each other too strongly, the inner region brightens and the orange arrows flip outward to represent the added short-range support term. The steep 1/r^12 choice is just a convenient stand-in for that support mechanism, not a claim about twelve dimensions or a derived microscopic law.
